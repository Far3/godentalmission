A Smile for Ecuador.

This dental mission is being launched August 4-9, 2015 in response to the lack of access to care and the resulting poor oral health of thousands of Ecuadorians. Free dental care will be provided to indigent children and their families in Guayaquil Ecuador. In addition to free treatment we will educate Ecuadorians about proper dental care and help them lead healthier lifestyles. Blanca House works to bring much needed quality medical care to countries and communities throughout Latin America working with medical personnel from America and Canada, they are able to provide both preventive and urgent health care to patients in outlying communities in South and Central America. Patients are served by Blanca House . This is an open collaboration among the health professions to assist in and encourage the organization of multi-disciplinary relief efforts in under-served international areas.

Blanca’s House Story

Started in 2008 by Suffolk County Nurse Anesthetist Galo Burbano, Blanca’s House works to bring much needed quality medical care to countries and communities throughout Latin America. Working with medical personnel from throughout America and Canada, we are able to provide both preventative and urgent healthcare to patients in outlying communities in South and Central America. Blanca’s House is different from other medical mission organizations. We offer patients a variety of medical procedures and surgeries in areas including gynecology, gastroenterology, plastic-surgery, general surgery, orthopedic surgery, dental care and surgery, audiological services, and ENT services.
